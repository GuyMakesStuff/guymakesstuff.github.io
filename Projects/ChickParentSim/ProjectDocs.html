Chicken Parent Simulator Is A Game Where You Must Go Out Into The Road And Collect Seed So You Can Feed Yourself And Your Babies, While Also Avoiding Incoming Cars And
Only Being Able To Carry Around 15 Seeds.

Development Of The Game Started On Late April 2021, When I Decided To Join The Jamulator 2021
Game Jam, A Week Long Competition On Itch IO Where You Must Make A Simulation Game And Publish It Before The Deadline. This Was My First Time Participating In An Online Game Jam, And I Joined It Because I Wanted To See If Joining A Jam Could Help My Games Get Noticed, And Also To Learn Some New Skills.



For My Game, I Decided To Play On The Classic "Why Did The Chicken Cross The Road" Line, And Decided That The Player Will Play As A Mother Chicken Risking Her Life By Going Into The Road To Collect Seeds For Herself And Her Babies.



I Worked very Hard On The Game, And Learned Many New Skills Along The Way Like Pixel Art,
Playing Audio Through An Audio Manager, Drawing Pixel Art, And More! In The End I, Ended Up
Releasing The Game 3 Days Before The Deadline On May 1st, And Although The Game Was Low Rated And Got Little Downloads, It Was The First Game I Have Recieved Proper Feedback On From Players, And I Got To Know That Some People Actually Played The Game And Liked It.



Overall, I Think That My First Time Doing A Game Jam Was Successful, And I Have Learned A Lot Along The Way!
